About Us

" We are a team of computer science students with a shared passion for coding and collaboration. Through the Code Battle Challenge, we aim to create a space where developers, regardless of their level, can come together to learn, solve problems, and grow. Our goal is to encourage innovation and teamwork, helping participants enhance their skills in a supportive and motivating environment. Join us on this journey of exploration and growth, where coding becomes a shared adventure!"

FAQ

  • What is CodeBattle?

    CodeBattle is an innovative programming competition focusing on creativity, innovation, and collaboration. Teams not only develop their own ideas but also improve and build upon the ideas of others.

  • Who can participate?

    The competition is open to students and young professionals in computer science, software development, or related fields.

  • When and where will it take place?

    The event will be held at the National School of Computer Science (ESI, ex. INI) in Algiers, from December 5 to December 7, 2024.

  • How can i register?

    Registration is available via link . Details regarding fees and procedures will be provided on the registration page.

  • How are teams formed?

    Participants can register individually or as a team. If you register individually, you will be assigned to a team on the first day of the event.

  • How are teams formed?

    Participants are free to use any tools and technologies of their choice, as long as they comply with the event rules.
